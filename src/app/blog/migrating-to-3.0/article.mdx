  # Introduction

  In the third version, Altrone was rewritten from the scratch. A new design system, new methods of working with components,
  and new component properties were introduced.

  To migrate to a new version you need to replace old components with new ones and change the logic of working with components in your application.

  Below we describe the components, hooks and utility functions and what alternatives exist in the new version
</Text.Section>
